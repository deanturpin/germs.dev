## ‚ÑπÔ∏è Notes on UK EV Towing

- **Braked towing capacity** applies to trailers with their own braking system.
- **Unbraked trailers** are typically limited to **750 kg max**, often less.
- Many EVs are **not approved to tow at all** ‚Äî if no figure is published, assume **0 kg**.
- Expect **30‚Äì50% range reduction** when towing, depending on load and speed.
- Tow ratings can change between **single-motor vs dual-motor** variants.
