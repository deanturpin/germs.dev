## Days of the week
- M - ponedjeljak “the day which follows nedjelja“
- T - utorok
- W - srijeda
- T - četvrtak
- F - petak
- S - subota
- S - nedjelja “day with no work”

See [days of the week](https://www.learncroatian.eu/blog/days-of-week-croatian).
