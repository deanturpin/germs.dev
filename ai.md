# AI dev

Thoughts on developing with AI.

It's obviously a game changer for rapid prototyping but what about large projects?

I've been working with a UX person coding an events website, they generate _so much code_... but equally I have to spend a chunk of time unpicking the madness. It's like you asked 1000 people to design all of the components of a car in isolation.

So I think it's good for short bursts of activity but at some point you have to stop and see what's really happened. Nobody is review 10000 merge requests.
