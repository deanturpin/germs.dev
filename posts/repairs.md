# Watch repairs

## Servicing
All watches are serviced, which means:
- Dismantle
- Clean parts with Renata essence
- Oil jewels with Synt-A-Lube Moebius 9010
- Grease keyless works with Moebius 8300
- Clean dial with water
- Polywatch or replace crystal
